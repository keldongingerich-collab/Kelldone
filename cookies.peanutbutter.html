Peanut Butter Cookies

HOME

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of peanut butter
  2. 1 cup of sugar
  3. 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter, sugar, and egg until smooth. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy your delicious peanut butter cookies!
